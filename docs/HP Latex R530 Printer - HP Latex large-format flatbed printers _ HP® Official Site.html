Meet the HP Latex R530 Printer

The only compact all-in-one rigid and flexible HP Latex printer

  • Up to 64 in (1.6 m) wide boards
  • 2 in (5 cm) maximum media thickness
  • Speeds up to 258 ft²/hr (24 m²/hr) indoor
  • 3-liter ink cartridges (colors and white)
  • Single roll up to 121 lb (55 kg)
  • Optional accessories: HP Latex R Series 64-in Extension Tables, HP Latex R530 Lateral Pinch Rollers Kit

Disclaimers

  1. HP Latex R530 Printer requires less operational space than any conventional 4x8 ft flatbed printer, which typically occupies over 28 sqm. This allows for better space efficiency in customer facilities.
  2. Target customers for the HP Latex R530 Printer traditionally use a Print & Mount technique, using rigid material and sticking a self-adhesive vinyl on top. With the HP Latex R530 Printer, they would print directly on the rigid material, saving on average 250 m2 of self-adhesive vinyl per month, thus reducing the amount of material used and disposed of.
  3. UL ECOLOGO® Certified HP 832 and 873 Inks meet a range of stringent human health and environmental considerations. For certifications, see https://www.ul.com/EL and https://www.ul.com/gg.

Check the complete list of compatible models at https://www.hp.com/go/printos. HP PrintOS Production Hub premium features are only accessible when purchasing an HP Professional Print Service Plus Plan.
  6. The HP Sustainability Amplifier program helps customers reduce their environmental impact through self-assessment, resources, and recognition with an HP badge, not an official eco-label. Recognition is subject to approval for credibility. Program is only available in English.
  7. The new maintenance cassette has at least 50% more life (liters of inks) thanks to a 50% longer cleaning cloth and to the new ink collection unit that helps use 100% of the cleaning cloth for all users compared to previous model available in HP Latex 700/800 Printer Series.
